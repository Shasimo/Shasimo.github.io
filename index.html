Introduction

What are Portalgons? First let us define some basic notions of Portalgons. Portalgons are essentially polyhedral structures which are flattened on the 2D plane by a series of fragments where any edge can be part of a so called portal. A portal is a couple of oriented edges that define a communicating pair of fragments. A fragment is any cut in your initial polygon representation which subtracted, creates portals along the edges your cut created, where any portal shares one side on the freshly cut piece, and your cut polygon. We here limit ourselves to 1-happy-portalgons
